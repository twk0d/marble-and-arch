About the Property

A masterpiece of modern architectural design, The Glasshouse offers an unparalleled living experience nestled in the pristine mountains of Telluride. This newly constructed estate seamlessly blends the rugged beauty of its surroundings with refined, contemporary minimalism. Expansive floor-to-ceiling windows ensure that every room is flooded with natural light while providing uninterrupted, panoramic views of the alpine landscape. The interior features bespoke finishes, including imported Italian marble, custom white oak cabinetry, and state-of-the-art smart home integration. Designed for both intimate living and grand-scale entertaining, the property includes a temperature-controlled wine cellar, a private wellness spa, and an infinity-edge pool that appears to spill into the valley below.
