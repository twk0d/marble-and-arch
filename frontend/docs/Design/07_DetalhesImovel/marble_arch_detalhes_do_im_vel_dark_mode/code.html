About the Property

Nestled upon the rugged coastal cliffs of the Riviera, The Glass Pavilion stands as a testament to uncompromising modern architecture and refined luxury. This masterpiece seamlessly blends the boundaries between indoor and outdoor living, offering panoramic, unobstructed views of the azure Mediterranean Sea from nearly every vantage point.

Meticulously crafted with a palette of warm, locally sourced masonry and extensive structural glass, the residence exudes a sense of serene permanence. The open-concept living spaces are bathed in natural light, featuring bespoke millwork, imported stone flooring, and smart-home integration that remains entirely invisible to the eye.

The primary suite is a sanctuary unto itself, occupying a dedicated wing with a private terrace, dual spa-like en-suite bathrooms, and expansive dressing rooms. Outside, the zero-edge infinity pool appears to spill directly into the ocean, flanked by manicured gardens and multiple alfresco entertaining areas, creating the ultimate private retreat.
