Frequently Asked Questions

Marble & Arch employs a rigorous verification process for all prospective buyers to maintain the exclusivity and privacy of our listings. Upon initial inquiry, our concierge will guide you through the submission of necessary financial documentation.

Properties listed on our platform must meet specific architectural and locational criteria. Our valuation process is conducted by senior appraisers who factor in global market trends, historical significance, and unique design elements.

We operate on a transparent fee structure. Platform access for browsing public listings is complimentary. Specialized legal consulting for international transactions and trust formations is billed separately through our partner network.
